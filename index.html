ABOUT US
Your comfortable stay in heart of Kalimpong

Hotel Oasis is a comfortable, budget-friendly stay located on D.S. Gurung Road, just a 2-minute walk from the Kalimpong bus stand. Our convenient central location makes it easy to explore local markets, nearby attractions, and business areas without long travel times.
We offer clean, cozy rooms with essential amenities, designed to give you a relaxing and pleasant stay—whether you’re traveling with family, on business, or visiting solo. At Hotel Oasis, we focus on comfort, value, and a peaceful atmosphere where you can truly unwind.
More than just a place to stay, Hotel Oasis is your welcoming home in Kalimpong, and we look forward to making your visit comfortable and memorable.
